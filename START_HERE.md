# AI/ML PhD Vault

This vault is organized around the research loop for an AI/ML PhD:

papers -> ideas -> hypotheses -> experiments -> results -> manuscripts

## Main Folders

- [[00 Inbox]] for quick capture
- [[01 Concepts]] for methods, theory, and technical concepts
- [[02 Literature]] for paper notes
- [[03 Ideas]] for early research ideas
- [[04 Projects]] for project hub notes
- [[05 Experiments]] for datasets, models, runs, evaluations, ablations, and error analysis
- [[06 Results]] for distilled findings, result summaries, and figure-ready outputs
- [[07 Meetings]] for advisor, group, and collaboration meetings
- [[08 Manuscripts]] for papers, thesis material, and review responses
- [[09 Presentations]] for talks, posters, and conference material
- [[10 Teaching]] for lectures, training, and student supervision
- [[20 Research Log]] for daily research logs
- [[70 People]] for advisor, collaborator, and contact notes
- [[80 Admin and Funding]] for admin, funding, and bureaucracy
- [[88 Templates/Vault Templates]] for active note templates
- [[99 Attachments]] for PDFs, figures, images, and exported artifacts

## Suggested Workflow

1. Capture rough thoughts in [[00 Inbox]].
2. Promote promising ideas into [[03 Ideas]] using the research idea template.
3. Turn ideas into testable hypotheses.
4. Plan experiments before running code.
5. Record every training run with commit, command, config, dataset version, and seeds.
6. Summarize results into project notes and manuscript outlines.
